WHO WE ARE


Elite Decor's origin dates back to 1990 when the company’s specialty was laying stone and ceramic in the residential sector. At first, it was a "one man show" featuring Mister Pépin, the father. In 1994, the enterprise became a family affair with the arrival of his son Jonathan, eager to learn the profession. Jonathan’s passion resided in custom home renovation, and he also had strong commercial experience. Together they then steered the company towards interior designing and planning. As of today we are a team of planners, architects, landscape architects and interior designers headquartered in New York and San Francisco with a network of domestic and international offices, with over 26 years of experience.

WHAT WE DO


Our Company offers a variety of services from interior design to architectural visualization. We have close relationships with big name companies located around the world. What's great is that we accept input from our clients and learn from it. We create designs in a collaborative environment enabling us to deliver the client’s ultimate vision. We are able to offer integrated services for your residential and commercial projects and we manage the plan development and interior design. We have always had a love for design and decorating and carry unique pieces to design your space, but also have specialists on hand to help you design and create the plan. You can check out some of our high end projects on the website in a few areas of specialty.

WHAT MAKES US SPECIAL


Our designs are current, yet still possess a timeless style generating broad market appeal. It is our passion to constantly pursue fresh inspiration. Our designers continually search for the most compelling design styles and highest quality of materials available. Hours of extensive market research allow us to create each design specific to your target market. We take pride in our work ethic, our eye for innovative designs, and our commitment to our clients. Our group of designers handle each job as a place and not a project. They work together in different ways that compliment and create a unique design experience, where we add a style that is colorful and bold to the spaces we design. The goal of our designs is to be fresh, productive and connect those within them successfully.
